TidalPool — Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 27, 2026

Store Listing Alignment

Checked June 27, 2026. This page was compared with the public public store listings where available. Where a store privacy or data-safety label was visible, it said the developer does not collect user data.

Accurate app behavior: Journal entries, photos, favorites, selected stations, settings, and optional location use stay local. Tide predictions are fetched directly from NOAA or Canadian Hydrographic Service providers; store systems handle purchase, restore, refund, receipt, or price display where applicable. No accounts, ads, analytics, tracking, or developer backend are used.

TidalPool collects nothing. It has no account, no analytics, no advertising, no tracking, and no third-party SDKs. There is no backend server; the bundled tide-station and species data and anything you save stay on your device.

Data stored on your device

Your journal entries (sightings, notes, and any photos you add), saved favorites, and settings are stored only on your device. They are never transmitted to us or anyone else. Deleting the app removes them. When you share a journal photo, its embedded GPS location is stripped before sharing.

Location

If you allow it, TidalPool uses your location only on your device — to find the nearest tide station and the species relevant to your area. Your location is never stored or uploaded.

Internet

The app works offline from bundled data. When you are online it fetches tide predictions directly from public government services — NOAA CO-OPS (United States) and Fisheries and Oceans Canada / the Canadian Hydrographic Service (Canada). No personal identifiers are sent. Tide predictions are for reference only and are not for navigation.

Purchases

TidalPool starts with a 3-day full-access trial and then uses a one-time unlock for continued access where distributed through an app store. Starting the trial happens locally on your device. Apple App Store or Google Play handles purchase, restore, receipt verification, refunds, and localized price display. TidalPool receives only the entitlement status needed to unlock the app; we do not receive your payment card, billing address, Apple ID, Google account details, or store receipt contents.

Reference data

Tide data is from NOAA CO-OPS and the Canadian Hydrographic Service (public domain / open data). This product includes data sourced from the Canadian Hydrographic Service of the Department of Fisheries and Oceans. Species information is compiled from public sources. Tidal Pool is not affiliated with or endorsed by NOAA, Fisheries and Oceans Canada, or any agency.
