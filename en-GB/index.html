Purchases

TidalPool is free to download with 3 days of full access. After that, a single optional one-off purchase unlocks the whole app permanently — there is no subscription, and nothing auto-renews or auto-charges. The App Store (iOS) or Google Play (Android) handles the purchase; TidalPool never sees your name, payment card, or billing details. Starting that 3-day access window happens locally on your device and does not create a subscription or charge; the store is contacted only if you choose to buy or restore the unlock.
