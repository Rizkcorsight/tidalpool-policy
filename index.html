TidalPool

Time the coast. Meet the residents. Leave no wake.

TidalPool is an offline tidepooling companion: when the next safe low tide is, the tide windows ahead, and the species you might meet — with a look-don't-touch ethic built in.

  • Next safe low tide + multi-day tide windows (NOAA + Canadian Hydrographic Service)
  • A field guide to tidepool species, with danger and first-aid notes for the few that bite or sting
  • A private journal of your finds — photos and notes stay on your device
  • United States & Canada · English & French

Start with a 3-day full-access free trial; after the trial, a one-time purchase unlocks TidalPool permanently. No account. No subscription. No ads. Nothing is collected — your finds stay on your device. Tide data is from NOAA CO-OPS and the Canadian Hydrographic Service. TidalPool is for reference, not for navigation, and is not affiliated with or endorsed by any agency.
